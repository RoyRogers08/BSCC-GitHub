General Comments

Computer Help Lab. Assistance for Windows computers is available every Wednesday 1 - 2:30 p.m. in the Senior Center's computer classroom. Based on the Best Buy Geek Squad's price list, we save each person helped an estimated $150.00.

Help Lab volunteers may be interested in your unwanted Windows laptop for recycling, if the operating system is Vista or later.

Project Reboot. This is a non-profit organization that provides computers at very little expense to individuals eligible for government assistance. The Bowie Seniors Computer Club is an authorized "referral agent." Go to their website or contact Dan Lee for information.

Members are encouraged to make presentations about topics they think might be of interest to the club. Your willingness to contribute will be appreciated by everyone. Contact Program Chairman, Dan Lee, to volunteer.

There is ample opportunity at most meetings for questions and shared experiences. Suggestions for new programs are always appreciated. Please feel free to pursue possible speakers yourselves.

Members are asked to send thanks to our speakers. We post the speaker's Email address on the white board at the front of the classroom.

These programs can show many different kinds of data on one map. This enables governments at all levels to more easily see, analyze, and understand patterns and relationships.
