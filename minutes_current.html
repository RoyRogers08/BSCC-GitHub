Minutes - Current Year Meetings

Meeting Schedule

Meetings are held the first Thursday of each month from 12:30 - 2:15 p.m. (unless that falls on a holiday).

Next meeting: Thursday, July 11, 2024

General Comments

Computer Help Lab.  Assistance for Windows computers is available every Wednesday 12:30 - 3:30 p.m. in the Senior Center's computer classroom. Based on the Best Buy Geek Squad's price list, we save each person helped an estimated $150.00.

Help Lab volunteers may be interested in your unwanted Windows laptop for recycling, if the operating system is Vista or later.

Members are encouraged to make presentations about topics they think might be of interest to the club. Your willingness to contribute will be appreciated by everyone. Contact the club president, Dave Hackenberg to volunteer.

There is ample opportunity at most meetings for questions and shared experiences. Suggestions for new programs are always appreciated. Members are encouraged to pursue possible speakers for meetings.

Members are asked to send thanks to our speakers. We post the speaker's Email address on the white board at the front of the classroom.
