Meeting Schedule

January 7:  Eric Hein will give a presentation on screen capturing.

Minutes - 2009

091119 – At the November 19th, 2009 meeting we discussed various computer problems of club members and their friends. Of particular interest were questions concerning the use of thumb drives on computers using USB 1.0 and 2.0.

091105 – At the November 5th, 2009 meeting we discussed some of the issues of upgrading to Windows 7. Dick Henthorn suggested that the club create a Google group where members could interact. Details for the annual Christmas Luncheon are outlined in the minutes. Make plans to attend on Thursday, December 17th.

091015 – At the October 15th, 2009 meeting Bob Blum demonstrated some of the features of Windows 7 and Norm Bohrer discussed some of the reviews to date and why XP users may wish to hold off on upgrading to this new operating system. Microsoft began selling Windows 7 to the general public on October 22nd.

091001 – The annual election of officers took place at the October 1, 2009, meeting. The slate of candidates was elected. All officers except the Vice President - Program Chairman agreed to serve another term. The club seeks a volunteer to fill this important vacant position.
