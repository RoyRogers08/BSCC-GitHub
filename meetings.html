Meeting Schedule

Meetings are held the first Thursday of each month from 12:30 - 2:15 p.m. (unless that falls on a holiday).

Next meeting: Thursday, July 11, 2024

Members are strongly encouraged to make presentations at meetings. Contact the club president, Dave Hackenberg, to volunteer.

There is ample opportunity at most meetings for questions and shared experiences. Suggestions for new programs are appreciated. Members are encouraged to pursue possible speakers for meetings.

Computer Help Lab

Assistance for Windows computers is available every Wednesday 12:30 - 3:30 p.m. in the Senior Center's computer classroom. Based on the Best Buy Geek Squad's price list, we save each person helped an estimated $150.00.

Help Lab volunteers may be interested in your unwanted Windows laptop for recycling or refurbishing if the operating system is Windows Vista or later.
